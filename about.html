About

WeCare is a non-profit Organization founded by four budding Zimbabwean technocrats, who are passionate about making a positive impact in all communities by striving to achieve or attain lasting solutions to an already dire situation within the communities in the face of difficulty and resistance.

Our mission is to be the helping-hand for those who find themselves faced with dilemma's that could ultimately take a toll on them if assistance is not readily accessible.
